About SAI Energy Services

Your Trusted Partner for Industrial Energy Savings

SAI Energy Services (SES) is a Pune-based energy management consultancy dedicated to helping industries reduce their energy costs through scientific audits, actionable recommendations, and full implementation support.

  • Led by Mr. Kailash Tarde — BEE Certified Energy Auditor with over 20 years of field experience across large-scale industrial plants
  • Trained in Japan under the "Energy Conservation Techniques for India" program — bringing international best practices to your plant
  • Conducted energy management training through ACMA, TERI, and Institute of Sustainable Communities for auto-component and forging clusters in Pune & Kolhapur
  • Experienced with Rolling Mills, Pharma Plants, Textile facilities, Foundries, Auto-component units, and more
  • Association with a network of Energy Auditors and Engineers for comprehensive multi-discipline studies
