Case Study 01

Pharma Cluster— Comprehensive Energy Audit

A large pharmaceutical manufacturing facility engaged SAI Energy Services for a full detailed energy audit. The facility had a complex energy profile combining electricity, furnace oil, and diesel. SES identified ₹2.69 Crore in annual savings — 9% of the total energy bill — with recommendations spanning immediate to 12-month payback periods.

⚡ Key Recommendations Identified

8 Boiler efficiency improvement using Air Pre-Heater

📡 Key Monitoring Parameters Recommended

In addition to one-time savings, SES recommended ongoing monitoring protocols to sustain and build upon the gains achieved:

🔥
Boiler Steam-to-Fuel Ratio

Monitor O₂ and CO in flue gas periodically to maintain optimal combustion efficiency.

💧
Water Balance Monitoring

Daily tracking of steam use, condensate return, and make-up water consumption.

❄️
Chiller kW/TR Ratio

Monitor chiller performance ratios to detect efficiency degradation early.

💨
Compressed Air Systems

KW/cfm monitoring at compressor level to identify leaks and optimize load management.

⚡
DG Set kWh/Litre

Monitor fuel efficiency of DG sets and benchmark against manufacturer specifications.

⚡
Plant level and equipment level energy monitoring

KWH/tonne , SCM/tonne , Kg/tonne , etc monitoring for key processes to track energy intensity and identify anomalies.

🌡️
AHU Simultaneous Heating & Cooling

Regular observation of AHUs to prevent energy waste from conflicting temperature control.

⚙️
Harmonic Filter Performance

Check harmonic filters at compressors with VFD, chillers with VFD, and other major harmonic sources.
