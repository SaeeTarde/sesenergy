Who We Are

Energy Conservation Backed by Expertise

SAI Energy Services (SES) is a specialist energy management and environmental services firm headquartered in Katraj, Pune. Founded on the principle that every industry — no matter how well-managed — has untapped energy savings potential, SES delivers systematic, instrument-based energy audits and management consulting to industrial clients across India.

Cost reduction is the need of the hour for every industry facing global competition. Energy — whether electricity, fuel, gas, steam, or captive generation — is one of the most impactful areas to target. SES has consistently demonstrated that 5–30% of annual energy costs can be saved through correct identification and disciplined implementation of conservation measures.

  • Established track record across 6+ industry sectors in India
  • Advanced software tools for energy modeling and assessment
  • State-of-the-art instruments including thermal imagers, flue gas analyzers, ultrasonic leak detectors, and power quality meters
  • Project implementation support available through group company network

A Career Dedicated to Energy Efficiency

Mr. Kailash Tarde brings over 20 years of hands-on experience in energy efficiency, energy management, and comprehensive energy audits across diverse industrial sectors. With a B.E. in Chemical Engineering and an MBA, combined with BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) certification from the Government of India, he leads every audit with deep technical knowledge and a practical, results-focused approach.

He attended a prestigious one-month training program on "Energy Conservation Techniques for India" held in Japan, bringing world-class methodologies back to Indian industry — a rare combination that sets SAI Energy Services apart from local consultancies.

Mr. Tarde has also served as Cluster Team Leader for Energy Efficiency projects under the Pune Forging Cluster and Mahindra Vendors, and has conducted energy management training programs through organizations including ACMA, TERI, and the Institute of Sustainable Communities.
